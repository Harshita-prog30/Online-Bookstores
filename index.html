Famous Authors

Munshi Premchand

His works like Godaan, Nirmala, and Sevasadan reflect the harsh realities of Indian society, while portraying the struggles of farmers, women, and the poor with deep empathy.

Known for his simple yet powerful style, Premchand raised social issues such as poverty, caste, and injustice in a way that continues to resonate even today.

Harivansh Rai Bachchan

The poet of *Madhushala*, he gave Hindi literature a unique lyrical style.
